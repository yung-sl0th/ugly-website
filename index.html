The ETERNAL in Spanish Art

Spanish art has long explored the idea of rebirth, decay, and the endless return. From Goya’s haunting reflections on conflict, to Dalí’s dreamlike loops of time, to Gaudí’s stunning buildings, each artist contributes to the idea of the eternal not as static, but as strange and cyclical.
